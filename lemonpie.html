Descripcion

Lemon meringue pie is a dessert pie consisting of a shortened pastry base filled with lemon curd and topped with meringue. This lemon meringue pie is so delicious and perfect for sharing! The sweet and tart lemon filling is thickened with cornstarch, flour, and egg yolks.

Ingredients

For the filling

  • white sugar
  • all-porpuse flour
  • cornstrach
  • salt
  • water
  • beaten eggs yolks

For the meringue

  • eggs whites
  • white sugar

Step by step

For the filling

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in a saucepan, then add the water and lemon juice and zest.

  2. Bring to a boil, then stir in the butter.

  3. Place the egg yolks in a bowl and stir in half the sugar mixture

  4. Whisk this mixture back into the original mixture.

For the meringue

  1. Beat the egg whites until foamy.

  2. Add the sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form.

Bake the pie

  1. Pour the filling into the pie shell and top with meringue.

  2. Bake in the preheated oven until the meringue is golden brown.

  3. ENJOY!
